Insulin Drip Protocol NOT for DKA

Initiation of Infusion
  • Initiate insulin infusion if 2 consecutive BG>180 or 1 BG>200
  • Initiate insulin infusion at 2 units/hour
 Frequency of Monitoring
  • BG every 6 hours until insulin infusion initiated based on above criteria
  • BG every 30 minutes: BG greater than 200 mg/dl or less than 100 mg/dl or after drip is stopped or decreased by half (50%) or more
  • BG every 60 minutes: BG is 100 - 200 mg/dl
  • BG every 120 minutes: BG is 145 - 180, AND there is less than 15mg/dl BG variation over 4 hours and insulin drip rate remains unchanged for 4 hours. If any change in BG more than 15 mg/dl or any change in insulin rate more than 0.5 units, return to checking BG every 30-60 minutes
  • During initiation of, rate change of, or cessation of any nutritional support (i.e. tube feeds or TPN), check BG every 30 minutes X 4.
